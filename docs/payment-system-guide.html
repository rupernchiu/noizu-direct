Part 1 — The Big Picture

Noizu Direct is a marketplace where creators (artists, makers, digital sellers) sell products to buyers. Money flows through the platform. We take a cut, release earnings to creators after a safety window, and pay them out in their local currency.

🛒 Buyer Pays

Buyer checks out using their card or Google Pay. We collect the money via Airwallex (our payment processor).

💰 Creator Earns

Creator's share sits safely in the platform. After the escrow window clears, it's ready for payout every Friday.

// Simple money math:
Buyer pays → Gross Amount
− Processing fee (Airwallex charge to us)
− Platform fee (our commission)
= Creator's Net ← what the creator actually earns
Part 2 — Payment IN (Buyer Pays)

How a buyer completes a purchase

  1. Buyer adds to cart & checks out They pick a product, click Buy, and reach the checkout page.
  2. Currency is detected automatically The platform detects the buyer's region and suggests the matching currency (e.g. MYR for Malaysia, SGD for Singapore, USD as default).
  3. Live FX rate is fetched We call Frankfurter API to get the real USD → local currency rate. The buyer sees the price converted live.
  4. Payment widget loads (Airwallex Drop-in) The buyer sees a payment form from Airwallex — card details, Google Pay, and eventually local methods like FPX (Malaysia) or PayNow (Singapore).
  5. Buyer pays Airwallex processes the payment securely. We never touch raw card numbers — Airwallex handles PCI compliance.
  6. Webhook fires & order is created Airwallex sends us a webhook saying "payment confirmed". Our server creates the Order and Transaction records in the database.
  7. Funds go into Escrow The transaction is marked ESCROW. Money is held for a safety window (configurable — e.g. 7 or 14 days) before being released to the creator.
  8. Escrow clears → COMPLETED After the window passes, the transaction moves to COMPLETED. The creator's net amount is now available for payout.

⏳ What is Escrow and why do we use it?

Escrow is a temporary hold on funds. If a buyer disputes a charge or requests a refund within the window, we can return the money without chasing the creator. Once the window passes safely, the money is released. Think of it like a cooling-off period before the creator can take the money out.

Part 3 — Payment OUT (Creator Gets Paid)

How creators receive their earnings

  1. Creator checks their Earnings dashboard Shows Available Balance, In Escrow, Total Earned, and Total Paid Out. All updated in real-time.
  2. Creator sets their payout currency (one-time setup) In their Profile settings, the creator selects what currency they want to receive (e.g. MYR, SGD, IDR, USD). This is locked until they change it again.
  3. Every Friday at 10am Malaysia Time — automated sweep runs A scheduled job (GitHub Actions → our server) automatically checks all creators with cleared earnings.
  4. Minimum threshold check: USD 10 If a creator's available balance is less than USD 10 (or equivalent), the payout is skipped that week and accumulates for next Friday.
  5. Payout is calculated & sent via Airwallex We call Airwallex's transfer API. The creator's USD earnings are converted to their chosen currency at the live rate and sent to their bank.
  6. Creator receives a payout email Notification sent with the amount and status. Payout appears in their dashboard history.
  7. Reconciler runs & updates status A second job checks Airwallex for the transfer outcome and marks payouts as PAID, PROCESSING, or FAILED accordingly.
// Payout calculation:
Available = Total COMPLETED earnings − Total already paid out

// Minimum check:
If Available < USD 10 → skip, roll over to next Friday

// Currency conversion:
Payout Amount = Available (USD) × Live FX rate → Creator's currency

✅ What counts as Available

Transactions in COMPLETED status only. Escrow transactions are not included until they clear.

⏳ What's still in Escrow

Transactions in ESCROW status. Visible on the earnings dashboard but not payable yet.

Monthly Statement

Creators can view a full monthly breakdown at Dashboard → Statement:

  • Every sale, fee, and net amount — grouped by month
  • Every payout — grouped by the month it was requested
  • Toggle between USD and their local payout currency
  • Download as CSV for their accountant

Part 6 — Deploy Checklist

When you are ready to go live, do these steps in order:

  1. Deploy to Vercel Push triggers auto-deploy, OR go to Vercel dashboard → noizu-direct → Deployments → Promote to Production.
  2. Verify all Vercel environment variables are set Check: AIRWALLEX_API_KEY, AIRWALLEX_CLIENT_ID, AIRWALLEX_WEBHOOK_SECRET, CRON_SECRET, DATABASE_URL, NEXTAUTH_SECRET, NEXT_PUBLIC_AIRWALLEX_ENV=prod
  3. Update Airwallex webhook URL In Airwallex dashboard → Webhooks → update endpoint to https://noizu.direct/api/webhooks/airwallex
  4. Update GitHub Secret APP_URL GitHub → repo Settings → Secrets & Variables → Actions → APP_URL → update to https://noizu.direct
  5. Run the payout workflow manually to test GitHub → Actions → Weekly Payout Sweep → Run workflow. Check logs for 200 OK.
  6. Make a test purchase with a real card (small amount) Verify: payment succeeds → order created → transaction in ESCROW → email received.
  7. Contact Airwallex to enable FPX and PayNow Provide your merchant account ID. They activate within a few business days. No code change needed.
⚠️ Before going live with real payments: Make sure NEXT_PUBLIC_AIRWALLEX_ENV is set to prod in Vercel (not demo or sandbox). Sandbox payments are test-only and will not process real money.
