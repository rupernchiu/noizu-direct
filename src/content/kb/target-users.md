## Two-sided marketplace

We serve buyers and creators. Both have to win for the platform to work.

## Creator personas

### 1. The active SEA cosplayer
- Based in Malaysia, Singapore, the Philippines, Indonesia, or Thailand.
- Sells convention photo sets and character print sets a few times a year.
- Currently uses Carousell DMs, Telegram, or Twitter for orders. Hates collecting payment manually.
- **What we offer them:** a checkout page with FPX/DuitNow/local rails, automated escrow, no chasing.

### 2. The doujin / artbook author
- Sells limited print runs (50–200 units) at NoizuCon, AFA, or via DMs the rest of the year.
- Wants international buyers but can't accept SWIFT or set up Stripe in MY/PH.
- **What we offer them:** a public storefront, card acceptance, payouts to local bank.

### 3. The full-time digital artist
- Sells wallpapers, character sheets, brushes, references.
- Used to Gumroad / Booth, but FX cuts and MY/SG-unfriendly payouts hurt.
- **What we offer them:** instant-download infra, USD pricing with local-currency display, lower payout friction inside SEA.

### 4. The commission artist
- Takes 1-of-1 commissions, scopes vary, payment is a trust problem.
- **What we offer them:** structured commission flow with milestones, escrow-held deposits, dispute fallback.

## Buyer personas

### 1. The local cosplay/doujin fan
- 18–35, MY/SG/ID/PH/TH, follows creators on Twitter and IG.
- Already DM-buys from creators; would rather pay with FPX or e-wallet than transfer manually.
- **What we offer them:** checkout that accepts the rail they actually use.

### 2. The diaspora / international fan
- Cares about specific creators they discovered online.
- Wants to pay in card, get tracking, have recourse if the package never arrives.
- **What we offer them:** card payment with 3DS, escrow protection, dispute path.

### 3. The convention buyer (catch-up)
- Saw a creator at a con, missed buying, wants the merch a week later.
- **What we offer them:** discoverable storefront for every creator.

## Who we are NOT for

- **Re-sellers and aggregators.** Listings must be the creator's own work; KYC + agreements enforce this.
- **Mass-produced anime merch shops.** Plenty of marketplaces serve them; we'd dilute the direct-from-creator value prop.
- **Adult-only platforms.** The category framing is general-audience cosplay/doujin/art. Adult content rules are TBD; not a launch focus.

## Geographic focus

- **Tier 1 (local rails enabled, tax registered or registering):** MY, SG, ID, PH, TH
- **Tier 2 (card-only buyers, creators may sell):** rest of SEA + global card markets
- **Tier 3 (SWIFT payout corridor for creators):** VN, KH, MM, LA — higher payout minimums, USD 35 wire fee
